# Safe Time Control
Safely speed up and slow down Mindustry without glitching your units in singleplayer, and banning/rubberbanding yourself in multiplayer. Original by [sk7725](https://github.com/sk7725). 

Key Features
- remove glitched units (respawn allied air units at the first core of your team instead)
- disable Time Control in multiplayer (unless you're the host) so you don't ban or rubber band yourself
- prevents you from glitching your units by limiting the game speed to a fifth of your FPS

Supports `x1/256` ~ `x256`. Simply drag the slider to adjust the speed.
Click/tap on 'x#' to shrink the ui. Click/tap on the button to adjust the speed, hold to expand back to full size.

This mod is incompatible with the original, and will automatically disable it upon launch.
